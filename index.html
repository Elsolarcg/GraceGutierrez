Visual Documentation of a Destination Wedding in Bogotá

A visual documentation project focused on design, logistics, and process, capturing how a destination wedding comes to life in Bogotá through space, architecture, and execution.

This project is designed to create both high-end editorial content and a long-term visual archive for your brand as a wedding planner.

Project Understanding

This collaboration is centered on documenting the behind-the-scenes reality of a destination wedding in Bogotá, with a clear emphasis on:

  • Design execution and spatial transformation
  • Backend logistics and coordination
  • The creative process behind the event
  • Architectural and city elements that connect the project to Bogotá

The focus is intentionally not on guests or the couple, but on how the space, design, and planning come together — aligning with your background in architecture and your position as a planner.

To ensure clarity, production continuity, and proper resource allocation, the following payment structure applies.

A non-refundable booking fee of USD 500 is required upon proposal acceptance.

This payment secures the production dates, team availability, and pre-production planning. The booking fee is applied toward the total project value.

Pre-Production Payment

30% of the remaining balance is due no later than 5 days prior to the start of production.

This covers creative planning, coordination, scheduling, and logistical preparation.

Post-Event / First Delivery Payment

The remaining balance is due upon delivery of the first round of content following the event.

This ensures timely post-production flow and delivery of all agreed materials.

Payment Method

All payments are processed via Stripe through secure payment links. This ensures a secure, simple, and internationally accessible payment process.

  • Booking Fee: Stripe payment link will be provided upon acceptance
